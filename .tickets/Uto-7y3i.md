# Standardize font size and spacing in Clean Cards Own Kingdom Summary

The Own Kingdom Summary card in Clean Cards view has inconsistent font size, line spacing, and style compared to the rest of the Kingdom News Report. Same issue exists in Thievery Impacts and Spell Impacts cards where the font changes in the section listing impacts per op or spell.

## Design

Update CSS in ui.js or associated styles to ensure consistent font-family, font-size, and line-height across all Clean Cards sections. Specifically target Own Kingdom Summary, Thievery Impacts, and Spell Impacts cards to match the standard Kingdom News Report styling.

## Acceptance Criteria

All text in Own Kingdom Summary, Thievery Impacts, and Spell Impacts cards uses consistent font size, line spacing, and style matching the rest of the Kingdom News Report.
