# Advanced Settings: move Combined Summary toggle into Display Options

The 'Show Province News/Logs input for Combined Summary' checkbox currently renders at the top of the left column (before Sections) in both the Province Logs and Province News Advanced Settings views. It should live in the Display Options section on the right column alongside other toggles, not as a standalone group at the top of the left column.

## Acceptance Criteria

- In Province Logs Advanced Settings view, the Combined Summary toggle appears in the Display Options (right column) section, not the left column
- In Province News Advanced Settings view, the Combined Summary toggle appears in the Display Options (right column) section, not the left column
- Toggle behaviour is unchanged: checking it shows the secondary input section, unchecking hides it and clears the textarea
- No other Advanced Settings layout is affected
